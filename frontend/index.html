QUESTIONS

Frequently asked

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No — DermAI provides cosmetic skin analysis only. We can identify common concerns like acne, texture, and pigmentation from photos, but we are not a substitute for a board-certified dermatologist. If you have a serious or unusual skin condition, please see a doctor.

How accurate is the AI?

We use a state-of-the-art vision model analysing three angles simultaneously, which improves accuracy over single-photo approaches. Accuracy improves significantly with good lighting and no makeup. We're transparent that it's not infallible — our goal is to give you a solid starting point backed by evidence, not a perfect diagnosis.

Where do my photos go?

Your photos are sent to our AI for analysis and then immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or sell images. The analysis results are saved locally in your browser (localStorage) and never leave your device after that. You can clear them any time by clearing your browser data.

Why these products and not others?

We only recommend products containing ingredients that have been tested in Randomised Controlled Trials (RCTs) — the most rigorous form of clinical evidence. No brand partnerships influence rankings. Products are sorted by how well their active ingredients target your specific concerns, ranked by severity.

Do I need to pay or create an account?

No. DermAI is completely free and requires no account, email, or login. We earn a small affiliate commission if you choose to buy a product through our Amazon links — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the lights on without charging you.

What if my country isn't supported?

We currently support 18 Amazon marketplaces (US, UK, India, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Sweden, Poland, Japan, Singapore, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Mexico, Brazil). If your country isn't listed, we default to amazon.com. We're working on adding more regions.
