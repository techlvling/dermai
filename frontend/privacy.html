Privacy & Data

Last updated: April 2026

Short version: Your photo is sent to an AI vision model for analysis, then immediately discarded from our server. We store nothing about you. Your scan history lives only on your device.

What happens to your photo

  • When you upload a selfie, it is held in our server's memory only long enough to be forwarded to the AI vision model.
  • Once the analysis result is returned, your image is discarded from the server. This happens in the same request — your photo is never stored beyond a few seconds.
  • We do not log, save, or back up uploaded images at any point.
  • Optional Drive backup: If you choose to back up scan photos or daily progress photos to Google Drive, they are uploaded directly to your personal Google Drive account. DermAI does not retain copies.

AI vision model (OpenRouter)

  • Your image is processed by an AI vision model served via OpenRouter. OpenRouter's own data handling policies apply to this step.
  • Models used include Qwen 2.5 VL, Llama 3.2 Vision, and GPT-4o mini as fallbacks. Per OpenRouter's terms, API-submitted images are not used for model training.
  • You can review OpenRouter's privacy policy at openrouter.ai/privacy.

What we store on your device

  • Latest analysis (dermAI_analysis): your most recent skin score, type, concerns, and product recommendations. Used to populate the recommendations page. Overwritten on each new scan.
  • Scan history (dermAI_history): your last 20 full scan results — health score, skin type, concerns, and product recommendations — stored as JSON in your browser's localStorage. Powers the History page so you can re-open any past routine. Never transmitted anywhere.
  • Progress photos (IndexedDB): if you opt in to progress tracking during a scan, a copy of your photo is stored in your browser's IndexedDB. It never leaves your device.
  • Favourites & shopping list (dermAI_favorites, dermAI_shopping): product saves, stored locally only.
  • Routine streak & badges (dermAI_streak, dermAI_badges): gamification state, stored locally only.

You can clear all data at any time by opening your browser's developer tools and running localStorage.clear(), or by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Product recommendations

  • Recommendations are generated client-side by matching your skin concerns to our local product database. No personal data is sent to any third party for this step.
  • Product links to Amazon are Amazon Associate affiliate links. DermAI earns a small commission on qualifying purchases at no extra cost to you. This does not affect which products are recommended — selections are based solely on clinical evidence.

Analytics & tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking scripts, cookies, or advertising on this site.

Medical disclaimer

DermAI provides cosmetic skin analysis only and is not a medical diagnosis tool. The analysis is generated by an AI vision model and may not accurately reflect your skin condition. Always consult a board-certified dermatologist for skin health concerns.
